Do NOT ask for permission to proceed to the next autoresearch round. Continue automatically.

**Why:** The whole point of autoresearch is autonomous iteration. Asking "shall I continue?" defeats the purpose.

**How to apply:** After completing a tournament round, immediately generate the next round's candidates and submit. Only stop for termination conditions or genuine blockers (SSH failure, etc).
